Sourdough Pizza Crust
Total Time:
* Active:
* Inactive:
* Baking:
* Other:

Ingredients:
* 1 Cup Unfed Starter
* 1/2 Cup Warm Water
* 2 1/2 Cups Unbleached All Purpose Flour
* 1/2 Tsp. Yeast
** Might try 3/4 Tsp. Yeast
* 1 Tsp. Salt
* Pinch Garlic Powder

Step 1: Combine all ingredients in a bowl
Mix to combine, then knead until smooth.

Step 2: Let it Rise
Place the dough in a greased container and let it rise until almost doubled in size (~2-4 Hours)
** For a faster rise place in a worm spot or double the yeast.

Step 3: Split the dough
Divide the dough in half and place on oiled baking sheets.
Let it rest for 15 minutes.

Step 4: Begin to form the crusts
Press the dough out to the size you want.
If the dough tries to snap back let rest for another 15 minutes.

Step 5: Preheat oven
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.

Step 6: Add sauce to crust
Sauce the crust as you like.

Par-bake for 5 minutes.

Step 7: Add toppings
Add cheese and other toppings as desired

Bake for 5-7 minutes, until cheese has melted or desired doneness.

** These instructions are for thin crust pizza. **
